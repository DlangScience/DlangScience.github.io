    Module libcerf.im_w_of_x

    Compute complex error functions, based on a new implementation of Faddeeva's w_of_z. Also provide Dawson and Voigt functions.

    Functions

    NameDescription
    im_w_of_x(x)Uses methods similar to the erfcx calculation: continued fractions for large |x|, a lookup table of Chebyshev polynomials for smaller |x|, and finally a Taylor expansion for |x|<0.01.
